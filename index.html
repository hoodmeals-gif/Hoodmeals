Welcome to Our NeighborHood

A Gift &
A Blessing.

Hood Meals is owned and operated by Chef Tee — Chef Latisha Mortise, established March 6, 2012 in Berlin, New Jersey. We offer some of the best southern cuisine in all of South Jersey.

We use only the highest quality and freshest ingredients to deliver a flavor that is not only unique in style, but unmatched in taste. Bringing home cooked goodness to your door, one neighborhood at a time.

What does the name Hood Meals mean? It isn't just about top notch flavors — one of our main focus points is our neighborhood. So much that it's in the name.
